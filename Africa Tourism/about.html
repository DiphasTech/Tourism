Who We Are

Our Story

Since 2008

Born from a Love of Africa

Summit Horizons Travel began with a single Land Cruiser, two guides, and an unshakeable belief that Africa's wilderness deserved to be shared — thoughtfully, responsibly, and unforgettably.

Founded in Kampala by conservationist and guide Jonah Ssebugwawo, the agency grew from small group gorilla treks in Bwindi to a full-service safari operator covering six countries and four ecosystems. Today we are a team of 38 specialists, but the spirit that launched us — curiosity, care, and a deep respect for the land — has never changed.

We have taken honeymooners to sleep under Botswana's stars, walked grandchildren beside elephants in Amboseli, and guided solo photographers into the mist of the Virungas at dawn. Every journey carries our name, and we treat that as a privilege we will never take for granted.

Our Mission

To Connect People with Wild Africa — and Leave Both Better for It

We believe that travel, done right, is one of the most powerful forces for conservation and cross-cultural understanding in the world. Every safari we run contributes to community projects, anti-poaching initiatives, and habitat restoration across our partner regions.

A portion of every booking goes directly to the Summit Horizons Conservation Fund — supporting ranger training, school building, and wildlife corridor maintenance in Tanzania, Uganda, and Botswana.
